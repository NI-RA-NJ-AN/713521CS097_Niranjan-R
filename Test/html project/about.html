About Facebook

Facebook is a social networking service that allows you to connect with friends and family, share photos and videos, and stay updated with the latest news. Founded in 2004, Facebook has grown to become one of the largest social media platforms in the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to give people the power to build community and bring the world closer together.
